Our Story

E-Clean was founded in 2024 with a mission to provide high-quality cleaning services to homes and businesses. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results and exceeding customer expectations.

E-Clean is a platform that not only provides house and room cleaning services for users but also offers job opportunities for individuals who meet E-Clean's criteria, thereby creating a triangular collaboration between the users, the cleaners, and the owners of the E-Clean.
